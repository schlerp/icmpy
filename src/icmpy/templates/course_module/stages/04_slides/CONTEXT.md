# Slides

Produce a slide storyboard from the lesson draft.

## Inputs

- Layer 4 (working): `output/lesson_draft.md`
- Layer 3 (reference): `_config/voice.md`

## Process

1. Convert key points into slide titles and visuals.
2. Add speaker notes from the lesson draft.
3. Keep one idea per slide.

## Outputs

- `output/slide_deck.md` — slide storyboard.
