# Idea

Distill the topic and angle into a focused thesis statement.

## Inputs

- None beyond the topic, audience, and angle from the builder questionnaire.

## Process

1. Review the topic: {{ topic }}.
2. Decide what claim the essay will defend or explore.
3. Write a single-sentence thesis.

## Outputs

- `output/thesis.md` — the working thesis and any open questions.
